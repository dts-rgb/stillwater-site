Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your fishing memories are yours. We built Stillwater to keep it that way.

This Privacy Policy explains how Stillwater ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

What We Collect

Account: Email address, Sign in with Apple identifier.
Catches: Photos, GPS coordinates, species, weight, length, date, notes.
Device: Device type, OS version, app version.

How We Use It

We use your data to sync across devices, identify fish species via AI, display location names on maps, and process subscriptions.

Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party service providers to operate the app. These include cloud infrastructure and authentication services, mapping and geolocation services, payment and subscription processing, and AI-powered features. Each provider is contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Data Sharing

We don't sell your data. We share only with the services listed above to operate the app, or if required by law.

Your Choices

You can delete catches in-app. To delete your account, email us. You can disable location and camera access in your device settings.

Children

Stillwater is not for children under 13.
